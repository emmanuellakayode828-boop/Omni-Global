The Founder

I am Emmanuella Kayode, a student in the Class of 2028 and the founder of OMNI.

I started this project because high-quality study resources are often expensive or difficult to access. OMNI is a non-profit initiative designed to change that by providing essential academic tools to students in any location.

Our goal is to offer high-yield practice and clear information for all major subjects, including SAT and ACT preparation. We believe that every student deserves the chance to succeed, regardless of their background or where they are studying in the world.
